About Us

Welcome to AB InfoTech Solutions Pvt Ltd

ABIS is an established BPO headquartered in Pune City in India. ABIS provides outsourcing solutions to its global spread client across multiple verticals such as customer contact, Training & Placements, digital marketing, IT training & outsourcing. ABIS was established in 2020 with the idea of enhancing customer service and taking client relationship management to new heights, in the Indian market.

As a management consultant, We specialized in Recruitment & Operations. Our initial project was a Giving Internship to youngsters with weak communication & lack of confidence that started with the following phases: training/ development, improving soft skill & interview preparation.
